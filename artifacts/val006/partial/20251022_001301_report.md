The agency and terminal operations provide dedicated vessel support, marketing, documentation, terminal operations, stevedoring, special cargo handling, bunkering, and docking/repairs. |
| Brand Recognition | The Group has over 50 years of experience in agency and terminal operations, establishing itself as a well-established leader in the shipping logistics industry. The Group is the official Terberg Tractors Logistics and Industrial Distributor in Singapore and was appointed as agent by Grimaldi Lines for Singapore in 2024. It also has a strong independent Board with a formal process for director selection and ongoing training, including sustainability matters. | The Group has maintained a 50-year partnership as General Agents with Daiichi Chuo Kisen Kaisha (DCKK) in Singapore and Malaysia, reflecting longstanding relationships and respect for its core values and business etiquette. It has received a Certificate of Appreciation from Mitsubishi Elevator (Singapore) Pte Ltd and recognition from Mitsui O.S.K. Line's Auto Carrier Express (MOL ACE) for low accident rates and safe cargo handling. The Group was also named among Forbes Asia's 2023 'Best Under A Billion' list, acknowledging its performance among small and mid-sized publicly traded companies in the region. |
| Reputation Ratings | The Group has a comprehensive Code of Conduct and Ethics overseen by the Board, with zero incidents of corruption reported and a whistle-blower policy in force. It holds BizSAFE Star and BizSAFE 3 certifications for occupational health and safety and complies with ISM and MLC standards. The Group publishes a standalone FY2025 Sustainability Report covering material ESG factors, climate-related disclosures consistent with TCFD recommendations, and sustainability governance as required under Rule 711B of the Listing Manual. It reports on nine material ESG factors including economic performance, anti-corruption, energy consumption, climate and environment, waste management, occupational health and safety, training and career development, employee well-being, and equal opportunity. The Group's sustainability reporting process is subject to internal review but does not currently have external assurance. | The Group complies with multiple ESG factors identified through a materiality assessment, including economic performance, anti-corruption, energy consumption, climate and environment, effluents and waste management, occupational health and safety, training and career development, employee well-being and engagement, and career progression and equal opportunity. It maintains a whistle-blower policy with zero incidents of corruption, holds BizSAFE Star and BizSAFE 3 certifications for occupational health and safety, and adheres to international standards such as ISM and MLC. The sustainability reporting process is internally reviewed and aligned with TCFD recommendations, though it currently does not have external assurance. |
