It has no confirmed incidents of corruption or non-compliance fines in FY2025. | The Group maintains a comprehensive sustainability reporting process aligned with the Task Force on Climate-Related Financial Disclosures (TCFD) and complies with environmental regulations such as IMO 2020, EEXI, and CII. The Group's sustainability efforts cover nine material ESG factors, including economic performance, anti-corruption, energy consumption, climate and environment, waste management, occupational health and safety, training and career development, employee well-being, and equal opportunity. |
